CossiePlay Monthly Parties

On the first Saturday of every month we hold a “CossiePlay Party” at the town hall.
Tickets cost:
  • $15 for adults
  • $10 for ages 6-17
  • Kids under 6 are free
Each entry includes one non-alcoholic drink and a glow stick. Parties start at 6pm and run until 11pm. There is always a live DJ at the party (who takes requests) and dancing is common (but not required). Contact us Patrons can register to come to a party by emailing us. They should include:
  • Contact person name
  • Phone number
  • How many people are coming
Be sure to check out our party guidelines before attending one of out parties.
